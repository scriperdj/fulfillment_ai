# Carrier Service Level Agreements

## FedEx
- **Service tiers**: FedEx Ground (5–7 days), FedEx Express (2–3 days), FedEx Overnight (1 day)
- **Delivery guarantee**: Money-back guarantee on Express and Overnight services.
- **Coverage**: All 50 US states, Canada, Mexico, 220+ international destinations.
- **Weight limit**: Up to 150 lbs per package for Ground; 70 lbs for Express.
- **Penalty clause**: If delivery is late on guaranteed services, shipping cost is fully refunded to us.
- **Pickup schedule**: Daily pickup by 5:00 PM local time.
- **Contact**: Account Rep — fedex-rep@fulfillment.internal | 1-800-463-3339

## UPS
- **Service tiers**: UPS Ground (5–7 days), UPS 2nd Day Air (2 days), UPS Next Day Air (1 day)
- **Delivery guarantee**: Guaranteed delivery by end of business day for air services.
- **Coverage**: All 50 US states, 220+ countries internationally.
- **Weight limit**: Up to 150 lbs per package.
- **Penalty clause**: Service guarantee refund for late air shipments. Ground has no time guarantee.
- **Pickup schedule**: Daily pickup by 6:00 PM local time.
- **Contact**: Account Rep — ups-rep@fulfillment.internal | 1-800-742-5877

## DHL
- **Service tiers**: DHL Express Worldwide (3–5 days international), DHL eCommerce (7–10 days)
- **Delivery guarantee**: Time-definite delivery for Express Worldwide.
- **Coverage**: 220+ countries. Primary carrier for international shipments.
- **Weight limit**: Up to 70 kg per shipment for Express.
- **Penalty clause**: Credit issued for late Express Worldwide deliveries upon claim filing.
- **Pickup schedule**: Scheduled pickups; same-day for Express if booked before 2:00 PM.
- **Contact**: Account Rep — dhl-rep@fulfillment.internal | 1-800-225-5345

## USPS
- **Service tiers**: Priority Mail (2–3 days), First Class (3–5 days), Media Mail (5–8 days)
- **Delivery guarantee**: No hard delivery guarantee on any service tier.
- **Coverage**: All US addresses including PO Boxes, APO/FPO, and territories.
- **Weight limit**: Up to 70 lbs for Priority Mail; 13 oz for First Class letters.
- **Penalty clause**: No penalty clause. Used for low-value, non-time-sensitive shipments only.
- **Pickup schedule**: Daily pickup from warehouse mailroom by 3:00 PM.
- **Contact**: Business Rep — usps-rep@fulfillment.internal | 1-800-275-8777

## Performance Tracking
- Carrier on-time rates are reviewed weekly.
- Carriers dropping below 95% on-time rate trigger a performance review meeting.
- Carriers below 90% for two consecutive months may be replaced.
