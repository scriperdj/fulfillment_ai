# Refund Policy

## Eligibility Criteria
A refund may be issued under the following conditions:
1. Item not received within the guaranteed delivery window plus a 3-day grace period.
2. Item arrived damaged or defective (photographic evidence required).
3. Wrong item shipped (customer must return incorrect item before refund).
4. Order cancelled before shipment leaves the warehouse.

## Refund Windows
| Situation              | Refund Window         |
|-----------------------|----------------------|
| Item not received     | 30 days from order   |
| Damaged on arrival    | 14 days from delivery |
| Wrong item            | 14 days from delivery |
| Pre-shipment cancel   | Immediate            |
| Post-shipment cancel  | Upon item return     |

## Refund Types
### Full Refund
- Applies when the item was never received or is completely defective.
- Includes original shipping cost if delay was carrier's fault.
- Processed to original payment method.

### Partial Refund
- Applies for minor cosmetic damage where item is still functional.
- Ranges from 15% to 50% of item value based on damage severity.
- Offered as store credit or original payment method (customer's choice).

## Process Steps
1. Customer initiates refund request via support portal or customer service call.
2. Agent verifies order details and checks eligibility against policy criteria.
3. For damaged items: customer uploads photos; agent reviews within 24 hours.
4. Approved refunds are processed within 3–5 business days.
5. Customer receives email confirmation with refund amount and expected timeline.
6. For bank transfers, allow additional 5–7 business days for funds to appear.

## Exceptions
- Clearance items are eligible for exchange only, not refund.
- Personalized/custom items are non-refundable unless defective.
- Refund requests beyond the refund window require manager approval.
- Repeat refund requests (>3 in 90 days) are flagged for fraud review.

## Store Credit
- When offered as alternative to refund, store credit includes a 10% bonus.
- Store credit never expires and is transferable within the same account.
- Partial refunds default to store credit unless customer explicitly requests cash refund.

## Escalation
- If refund is denied and customer disputes, escalate to senior agent.
- Disputes unresolved after 48 hours escalate to operations manager.
